How to Add Posts:

Click the button below to open your Google Sheet and add new posts:

Open Google Sheet

Step-by-Step Guide:

  1. Open the Google Sheet (click button above)
  2. Add a new row at the bottom
  3. Copy this format: 
    id: 3 (auto-increment)
type: "slider" or "single"
images: "url1,url2,url3" (comma separated)
title: "Your title"
description: "Your description"
category: "photos" or "videos"
instagramUrl: "https://instagram.com/p/..." (optional)
date: "2024-01-20"
order: 3 (for sorting)
active: "TRUE"
  4. Save (auto-saves in Google Sheets)
  5. Refresh your gallery page to see changes

How to get Instagram Image URLs:

  1. Open Instagram post in browser
  2. Right-click on image → "Copy image address"
  3. Paste in Google Sheet
  4. For multiple images, copy each one and separate with commas
